## Research Hypotheses

| Hypothesis | Variables Tested |
| --- | --- |
| **H1** | Large companies differ significantly in DPP readiness compared to small companies. |
| **H2** | Top management support has a positive correlation with DPP readiness. |
| **H3** | Cost is the most significant barrier to adoption among suppliers. |
| **H4** | High concern regarding implementation costs is negatively correlated with adoption intent. |
| **H5** | Consumer demand for transparency influences readiness more strongly than government support. |
| **H6** | Organizational readiness is positively moderated by the readiness of supply chain partners. |
| **H7** | Companies with prior digital transformation experience are more likely to have a defined timeline for adoption. |
| **H8** | Regulatory awareness is positively correlated with budget allocation for digital initiatives. |
